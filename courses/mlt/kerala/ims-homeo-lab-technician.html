IMS Homeo MLT Exam Overview.

Conducted By
Kerala Public Service Commission (Kerala PSC)
Name of Examination
Kerala PSC IMS Homoeo MLT Exam
Course / Post
Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT), Indian Systems of Medicine / Homoeo
Recruitment Type
Merit-Based Recruitment through Written Examination
Eligibility Qualification
Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT), B.Sc Medical Laboratory Technology (B.Sc MLT), or another recognized equivalent qualification as prescribed in the recruitment notification
Registration Requirement
Valid professional registration with the Kerala Paramedical Council or another recognized authority, where applicable
Duration / Job Type
Permanent government service post as per applicable Kerala PSC recruitment rules
Mode of Application
Online through the official Kerala PSC Thulasi portal
Selection Process
Written Examination, Document Verification, Medical Fitness Verification, Final Appointment
Participating Institutions
Government Homoeopathic hospitals, dispensaries, laboratories, and healthcare institutions under the relevant Kerala Government department

01

Online Application Submission

Candidates apply through the official Kerala PSC Thulasi portal by submitting personal, educational, professional, and other required details.

02

Application Verification

Submitted applications are checked against eligibility criteria, qualifications, age limits, registration conditions, and other requirements stated in the Kerala PSC notification.

03

Written Examination

Eligible candidates appear for the Kerala PSC written examination based on the prescribed syllabus, including Medical Laboratory Technology subjects relevant to the notified post.

04

Shortlisting and Rank List Preparation

Candidates are shortlisted based on examination performance and applicable Kerala PSC ranking criteria.

05

Certificate Verification

Shortlisted candidates must produce original documents for verification of educational qualifications, professional registration, identity, category claims, and other required credentials.

06

Medical Fitness Verification

Selected candidates may undergo medical fitness verification according to applicable government service and departmental requirements.

07

Final Appointment

Final selection is completed based on rank list position, eligibility, certificate verification, medical fitness, reservation rules, and applicable Kerala PSC recruitment conditions.
