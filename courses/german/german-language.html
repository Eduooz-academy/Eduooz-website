Curriculum Roadmap

What You Will Learn.

A1 (Beginner)

Beginner Level

Basic greetings, introductions, numbers, daily routines, simple conversations, and essential grammar. Build your foundation to understand and use familiar everyday expressions.

A2 (Elementary)

Elementary Level

Expand vocabulary, form sentences about personal information, shopping, travel, and work. Start understanding short texts and dialogues and expressing yourself in simple terms.

B1 (Intermediate)

Intermediate Level

Gain confidence in talking about experiences, events, dreams, hopes, and ambitions. Learn to write simple connected text and understand the main points of standard conversations and texts.

B2 (Upper-Intermediate)

Upper-Intermediate Level

Communicate fluently with native speakers, discuss complex topics, write clear detailed text, and understand longer conversations, lectures, and articles.
